## Registration

### Step 1: Access Registration Page

1. Click the **"Register"** button on the login page
2. Or navigate directly to: `https://your-domain.com/register`

### Step 2: Fill Registration Form

**Required Information**:

1. **Full Name**
   - Enter your complete name as it appears on your civil ID
   - Example: "Ahmed Mohammed Al-Rashid"

2. **Email Address**
   - Use a valid email address that you have access to
   - This will be your username for login
   - Must be unique (not already registered)
   - Example: "ahmed.rashid@example.com"

3. **Civil ID**
   - Enter your 12-digit Kuwait civil ID number
   - Numbers only, no spaces or dashes
   - Must be unique (not already registered)
   - Example: "123456789012"

4. **Mobile Number**
   - Enter your 8-digit Kuwait mobile number
   - Numbers only, no spaces or country code
   - Must be unique (not already registered)
   - Example: "98765432"

5. **Password**
   - Minimum 8 characters
   - Must contain:
     - At least one uppercase letter (A-Z)
     - At least one lowercase letter (a-z)
     - At least one number (0-9)
     - At least one special character (!@#$%^&*)
   - Maximum 64 characters
   - Example: "MyP@ssw0rd2024"

6. **Confirm Password**
   - Re-enter your password exactly as above
   - Must match the password field

7. **Pharmacy Selection**
   - Select the pharmacy you will primarily visit
   - You can select a different pharmacy when creating bookings

8. **hCaptcha Verification**
   - Check the "I'm not a robot" box
   - Follow any additional verification steps if prompted

### Step 3: Submit Registration

1. Review all entered information
2. Click the **"Register"** button
3. You will see a success message
4. Check your email for verification link

**Important Notes**:
- All fields marked with * are required
- Your password is encrypted and cannot be recovered (only reset)
- Keep your login credentials secure
- Do not share your account with others

### Common Registration Errors

**"Email has already been taken"**:
- This email is already registered
- Try logging in or use the "Forgot Password" feature
- Use a different email address

**"Civil ID has already been taken"**:
- This civil ID is already registered
- Contact support if you believe this is an error

**"Mobile number has already been taken"**:
- This mobile number is already registered
- Contact support if you believe this is an error

**"Password is too weak"**:
- Ensure your password meets all complexity requirements
- Try mixing uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters

**"hCaptcha verification failed"**:
- Make sure you checked the captcha box
- Try refreshing the page and completing captcha again
- Check your internet connection

## Email Verification

After registration, you must verify your email address before logging in.

### Step 1: Check Your Email

1. Open your email inbox (the one you registered with)
2. Look for an email from the booking system
3. Subject: "Verify Your Email Address"
4. **Check spam folder** if you don't see it in inbox

### Step 2: Click Verification Link

1. Open the verification email
2. Click the **"Verify Email Address"** button or link
3. You will be redirected to the system
4. You will see a success message

### Step 3: Wait for Admin Approval

After email verification:
1. Your account is created but **inactive**
2. An administrator must approve your account
3. You will receive an email when approved
4. This usually takes 1-2 business days

### Resending Verification Email

If you didn't receive the email:
1. Try to login with your credentials
2. You will be redirected to verification notice page
3. Click **"Resend Verification Email"**
4. Check your inbox again

## Creating a Booking

### Step 1: Access Booking Form

1. From your dashboard, click **"Create New Booking"**
2. Or click **"Bookings"** → **"New Booking"** from navigation

### Step 2: Select Pharmacy

1. Choose the pharmacy you want to visit
2. Only active pharmacies are shown
3. If your assigned pharmacy doesn't appear, contact support

### Step 3: Select Department

1. Choose the department you need to visit
2. Examples: Dermatology, Cardiology, General Medicine
3. Only departments of the selected pharmacy are shown
4. Only active departments are available

### Step 4: Select Date

1. Click on the date field to open the calendar
2. Select a date (today or future dates only)
3. Past dates are not selectable
4. The system shows available dates based on department schedule

### Step 5: Select Time Slot

1. After selecting a date, available time slots will appear
2. Only available slots are shown
3. Unavailable slots are:
   - Already booked
   - Past times (if today's date)
   - Outside department working hours
4. Click on your preferred time slot

### Step 6: Add Notes (Optional)

1. Add any additional information or notes
2. Maximum 500 characters
3. Examples:
   - "Bringing new product samples"
   - "Follow-up visit from last month"
   - "Need to discuss promotional offers"

### Step 7: Submit Booking

1. Review all information:
   - Pharmacy name
   - Department name
   - Date and time
   - Notes
2. Click **"Submit Booking"** button
3. You will see a success message
4. The booking status will be "Pending"

### After Booking Submission

1. **Confirmation Email**: You will receive an email confirmation
2. **Pending Status**: Your booking awaits pharmacy admin approval
3. **Notification**: You will be notified when booking is confirmed
4. **Dashboard**: Booking appears in your dashboard

### Booking Rules and Limitations

- **No duplicate bookings**: Cannot book the same department twice on the same day
- **Past times blocked**: Cannot select time slots that have already passed
- **Working hours only**: Can only book during department working hours
- **One slot at a time**: Only one representative per time slot (unless configured otherwise)
- **Cancellation policy**: Can cancel booking up to 24 hours before appointment

## Managing Your Bookings

### Viewing All Bookings

1. Click **"My Bookings"** from navigation
2. You will see a list of all your bookings

### Booking List Information

Each booking shows:
- **Pharmacy name**
- **Department name**
- **Date and time**
- **Status**: Pending, Confirmed, Cancelled, or Completed
- **Action buttons**: View details, Cancel (if applicable)

### Booking Status Types

**Pending**:
- Awaiting pharmacy admin approval
- You can cancel at this stage
- Admin will review and confirm/reject

**Confirmed**:
- Pharmacy admin has approved your booking
- Appointment is scheduled
- You can still cancel (with conditions)

**Cancelled**:
- Booking has been cancelled (by you or admin)
- Shows cancellation reason
- Cannot be reactivated

**Completed**:
- Appointment has been completed
- Past appointments are automatically marked as completed
- No actions available

### Viewing Booking Details

1. Click **"View Details"** on any booking
2. You will see:
   - Full booking information
   - Status history
   - Notes
   - Cancellation reason (if cancelled)

### Cancelling a Booking

**To cancel a booking**:
1. Go to **"My Bookings"**
2. Find the booking you want to cancel
3. Click **"Cancel"** button
4. Confirm cancellation
5. Optionally provide a cancellation reason
6. Click **"Confirm Cancellation"**

**Cancellation Rules**:
- Can cancel **Pending** bookings anytime
- Can cancel **Confirmed** bookings up to 24 hours before appointment time
- Cannot cancel **Completed** or already **Cancelled** bookings
- Multiple cancellations may result in account restrictions

### Filtering Bookings

Use filters to find specific bookings:
- **Status**: Filter by Pending, Confirmed, Cancelled, Completed
- **Date Range**: Filter by date range
- **Pharmacy**: Filter by pharmacy
- **Department**: Filter by department

### Exporting Bookings

1. Click **"Export"** button
2. Choose format (PDF or Excel)
3. Bookings will be downloaded to your device

## Troubleshooting

### Cannot Login

**Problem**: Invalid credentials error
**Solution**:
1. Check for typos in email and password
2. Passwords are case-sensitive
3. Use "Forgot Password" to reset
4. Clear browser cache and try again

**Problem**: Account not verified
**Solution**:
1. Check email inbox for verification link
2. Check spam folder
3. Click "Resend Verification Email"

**Problem**: Account pending approval
**Solution**:
1. Wait for admin approval (1-2 business days)
2. Contact support if urgent

### Cannot Create Booking

**Problem**: No time slots available
**Solution**:
1. Try a different date
2. Try a different department
3. Contact pharmacy to check availability

**Problem**: Department not shown
**Solution**:
1. Check if pharmacy is selected correctly
2. Department may be inactive
3. Contact pharmacy admin

### Not Receiving Emails

**Solutions**:
1. Check spam/junk folder
2. Add system email to contacts
3. Check if email address is correct in profile
4. Contact support to resend

### Page Loading Issues

**Solutions**:
1. Check internet connection
2. Refresh the page (F5 or Ctrl+R)
3. Clear browser cache
4. Try a different browser
5. Try incognito/private mode

### Mobile App Issues

**Solutions**:
1. Update your browser app
2. Clear app cache
3. Check if JavaScript is enabled
4. Try desktop version

## FAQs

### General Questions

**Q: Is the system free to use?**
A: Yes, the booking system is free for medical representatives.

**Q: Can I have multiple accounts?**
A: No, each civil ID can only be registered once.

**Q: Can I book for multiple pharmacies?**
A: Yes, you can create bookings for any active pharmacy, not just your assigned one.

**Q: How far in advance can I book?**
A: You can book appointments based on the pharmacy's schedule availability. Typically up to 30 days in advance.

### Account Questions

**Q: I forgot my password. What should I do?**
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions. You will receive a reset link via email.

**Q: Can I change my email address?**
A: No, you cannot change your email address yourself. Contact support for assistance.

**Q: How long does account approval take?**
A: Usually 1-2 business days. You will receive an email notification when approved.

**Q: Can I delete my account?**
A: Contact support to request account deletion.

### Booking Questions

**Q: Can I modify a booking after submission?**
A: No, you cannot modify a booking. You must cancel and create a new one.

**Q: What happens if I miss an appointment?**
A: The booking will be marked as "No Show". Multiple no-shows may result in restrictions.

**Q: Can I book the same department multiple times on the same day?**
A: No, only one booking per department per day is allowed.

**Q: How do I know if my booking is confirmed?**
A: You will receive an email notification when the pharmacy admin confirms your booking. You can also check the status in "My Bookings".

**Q: Can I cancel a confirmed booking?**
A: Yes, you can cancel up to 24 hours before the appointment time.

### Technical Questions

**Q: Which browsers are supported?**
A: Chrome 90+, Firefox 88+, Safari 14+, Edge 90+, and their mobile equivalents.

**Q: Is there a mobile app?**
A: The system is web-based and mobile-responsive. No separate app is needed.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes, the system uses industry-standard security measures including encryption, HTTPS, and secure authentication.

**Q: Can I use the system offline?**
A: No, an internet connection is required.

### Support Questions

**Q: How do I contact support?**
A: Email: support@example.com or Phone: +965 XXXX XXXX

**Q: What are support hours?**
A: Sunday - Thursday, 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM Kuwait Time

**Q: How quickly will I get a response?**
A: Support tickets are typically answered within 24 hours during business days.

## Glossary

- **Booking**: An appointment scheduled with a pharmacy department
- **Civil ID**: 12-digit Kuwait national identification number
- **Department**: A specific section within a pharmacy (e.g., Dermatology)
- **Time Slot**: A specific time window for appointments
- **Pending**: Booking awaiting approval
- **Confirmed**: Approved booking
- **Representative**: Medical sales representative (you)
